About Me

An exploration of my journey through astrophysics, quantum optics, and engineering.

Who I Am

I am a researcher passionate about pushing the boundaries of science at the intersection of astrophysics and quantum technologies. With a strong foundation in electrical engineering and an enduring fascination with the cosmos, I have carved out a niche in data analysis, high-precision instrumentation, and machine learning for advanced astronomical research.

Current Focus

As a researcher, my efforts are directed toward advancing our understanding of the universe by integrating modern technologies into astrophysical studies. My primary focus areas include:

  • Gravitational Wave Astronomy: Developing real-time parameter estimation tools for astrophysical mergers.
  • Quantum Optics: Innovating optical systems for improved astronomical observations.
  • AI in Astronomy: Leveraging machine learning to optimize data processing and modeling.
  • Exoplanet Science: Employing interferometry and adaptive optics to explore planetary systems.
